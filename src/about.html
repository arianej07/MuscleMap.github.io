About Our App

Welcome to the Muscle Map App! Our mission is to make fitness accessible and enjoyable for everyone, regardless of their experience level or workout environment.

Key Features:

  • Interactive muscle map for targeted exercise exploration
  • Comprehensive workout plans for various fitness goals
  • Progress tracking to monitor your fitness journey
  • Expert-designed exercises with detailed instructions
  • User-friendly interface for seamless navigation

Whether you're a beginner just starting your fitness journey or an experienced athlete looking to switch up your routine, our app provides the tools and information you need to achieve your health and fitness goals.

About the Developer

This app was created by Aria Nejad as a high school capstone project. It represents a culmination of my interests in fitness and web development, aiming to create a practical tool that can help people on their fitness journeys.

Our Commitment

We are committed to providing accurate, up-to-date fitness information and continuously improving our app based on user feedback and the latest exercise science research.
